Professional Statement

I am both a technical and creative individual. I thrive in both collaborative and indivuidualized settings. I am results driven and have a passion for obtaining new knowledge.

Artist's Statement

I find beauty in natural symmetry. I paint with watercolor to emphasize nature's beauty through light. I then use pen and ink to define the symmetry and distinct lines of and object. This style allows me to appreciate both the randomness and order found in the world.
