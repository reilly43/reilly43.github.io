    West Chester University

    I currently provide technical support for WCU's assessment software system, Tk20 by Watermark. Examples of my training videos as well as technical guides can be found on the WCU Tk20 by Watermark help site.The videos on this site were a result of me training myself on how to use Camtasia. From this I was able to create concise, technical documentation of common processes that students and faculty would perform. I have also been able to use these videos during in-person presentations as an additional resource.

    The College of New Jersey

    Recently, I had the opportunity to provide remote training and database solutions for The College of New Jersey's Office of Graduate and Advancing Education. I had the opportunity to redesign a database I had created when previously working there, prior to my current role at West Chester. This student database allows a user to input student information as well as toggle through applicants. The database was used to track applicants in a special program and for storing admissions information. I used MS Access to develop the database. This allowed the office to easily pull reports and keep track of enrollment data.
